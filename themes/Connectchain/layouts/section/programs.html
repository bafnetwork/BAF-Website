Education Programs

We believe knowledge is power, especially in an emerging industry. As such, it is crucial to connect people with open source educational resources and increase learning opportunities in the space -- and that’s just what we do. Take a look at some of our educational programs.

Accredited Courses

We collaboratively devise curriculum, source lecturers, and strategically advocate for the accreditation of blockchain courses. We started 4 accredited blockchain courses through computer science departments.

Web3 Teacher Training Track

The BAF Web3 Teacher Training Track (Web3TTT) is an intensive six-week training program for aspiring developers and educators in smart contract and dApp development.

Meetups

We aim to bring the space together and foster a culture of collaboration. Some of the ways we do this include Educational Meetups and Public Community Calls. In our weekly meetings, we highlight paradigm-shifting technologies, new protocols and diverse applications of blockchain while engaging our members in dynamic discussions with industry experts.

Recruiting

Think of us as a talent machine. We bridge the gap between our members and our industry through our recruiting program. We have point-of-contacts at our partner companies and we share open roles on our job board. We help our members with every step of the recruiting process till they find the right fit from resume reviews to mock interviews to referrals.
